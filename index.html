We chose to take this video which deals with the addiction of video games. We chose this video because we can see a young man of 20 years old who is addicted to video games. Having chosen this video allowed us to question ourselves because both of us are very addicted to video games and we can spend many hours playing video games such as 10 hours in front of the screen.We also started playing video games in libraries or Cyber ​​cafe.

Explanation

We are going to give a presentation on our subject. We are doing a 30 days challenge. The challenge is not to use your computer or other devices to play video games, watch movies, series, streams, so this is a complicated challenge for us who are addicted to video games.The challenge during the Christmas holidays and we have put in place a set of rules for this challenge.

A presentation of the subject

The list of rules that we have implemented:

-We use our computer or other devices only to work for 30 days.
-We start our computers at 1:00pm until 4:00pm.
-Prohibition to use your computer or other devices during the night.
-Return computers and cellphones to parents from 6:00pm.
- The internet connection is disconnected from 6:00pm
-Performing both sports and fun activities to relax.

A set of rules for this challenge

Our Experience report
We find that this challenge was what was needed.
For 30 days, it was a complicated challenge we were tempted to play video, to stay in front of the screens all night but we did not give in.
Moreover during this challenge, we did not have any pain in the eyes , we spend more time with the family, we are less nervous. We woke up more easily in the morning. In a nutshell, it was a wonderful experience and the rules that we put in place helped us for this challenge.
We will continue to respect this rules that we have established.
